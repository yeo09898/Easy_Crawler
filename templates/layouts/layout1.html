About WebCrawler

EasyCrawler is a free to use Web Crawler that can help you get the real-time result for the tags you entered from Twitter, Yelp, Stack Overflow.

Copyright © Your Website 2017

Step 1

Input the tags you want to search in the first inputbox. Multiple tags could be seperated by comma.

Step 2

Input the time you want to spend on searching the tags. It is counted by minute. At least one minute.

Step 3

Click "Start" to begin to searching data.

Step 4

After clicking "Start", the button will be disabled and it will show you the remaining time here.

Step 5

When the crawling is finished, you can click "Download" to download your data. Or clicking "Back" to return to index page to begin another new crawler.

Step 6

Welcome to send us feedback.
